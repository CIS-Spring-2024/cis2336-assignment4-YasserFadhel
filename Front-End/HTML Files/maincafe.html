ABOUT US

CoogEats is a vibrant cafeteria located at the University of Houston Sugar Land campus.
We pride ourselves on offering a diverse range of delicious and freshly prepared meals to cater to the diverse tastes of our patrons.
Our cafeteria is not just a place to dine; it's a hub of culinary excellence, where students, faculty, and visitors gather to savor mouthwatering dishes, socialize, and fuel their academic pursuits.
Whether you're looking for a quick bite between classes or a relaxing meal with friends, CoogEats is the perfect destination for a satisfying dining experience.
